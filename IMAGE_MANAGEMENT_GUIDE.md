# IMAGE MANAGEMENT GUIDE

## Overview
This guide provides detailed instructions on how to update and manage product images without the need to edit HTML directly. This ensures a smoother and safer process for updating images across your product listings.

## Steps to Update Product Images

1. **Access the Image Management Dashboard**  
   - Log in to the administrative dashboard where you manage product listings.
   - Navigate to the 'Product Management' section.

2. **Select the Product**  
   - Locate the product whose image you want to update.
   - Use the search bar or browse through the list of products to find it.

3. **Upload New Image**  
   - Click on the product to open its management page.
   - Look for the 'Images' section on the product page.
   - Click on the 'Upload Image' button.
   - Select the new image file from your computer. (Ensure it meets the required format: JPG, PNG, etc.)

4. **Set Image Alt Text**  
   - After uploading, you might have the option to add 'Alt Text' to the image. Fill this in for better SEO and accessibility.

5. **Save Changes**  
   - Once the image is uploaded and alt text added, make sure to click the 'Save' button to apply changes.

6. **Preview Changes**  
   - Go to the product page on your site to preview the updated image and ensure everything looks correct.

## Best Practices  
- Always use high-quality images that fit the required dimensions for optimal display.
- Regularly review and update product images to keep the content fresh and engaging.
- Maintain a consistent style for product images to ensure uniformity across your site.

## Troubleshooting
- If you encounter issues uploading images, check your internet connection or try reloading the page.
- Ensure that the image file size does not exceed the platform's limits.

## Conclusion
Following this guide allows for efficient image management without the need for HTML coding. Make image updates regularly to ensure your products are showcased effectively!
